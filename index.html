Scope

This policy applies to all utilities developed by PFSmarter and distributed on Apple’s App Store.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data. Our applications run locally on your device. Any data you input (e.g., calculator values) remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

  • Analytics/tracking: None.
  • Crash reports: None are sent to us. If you have opted into Apple's diagnostic settings, those are governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • Accounts/sign-in: Not required.
  • Payments: All payments are handled by Apple; we do not receive any of your payment card data.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no server-side retention. Any values you save within the app stay on your device until you delete the application or reset its data.

Third Parties

We do not integrate any third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. App distribution and purchases are provided by Apple. Please refer to Apple’s privacy policies for platform-level data.
